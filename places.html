Places to Visit in Bengaluru

1. Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh is a famous botanical garden in Bengaluru. It is home to a variety of exotic plants and trees, making it a great place for nature lovers.

2. Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a beautiful palace built in Tudor-style architecture. It offers a glimpse into the rich history of the city.

3. Cubbon Park

Cubbon Park is a large public park in the heart of Bengaluru. It provides a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Parks in Bengaluru

1. Bannerghatta National Park

Bannerghatta National Park is a wildlife sanctuary that hosts a variety of animals, birds, and plants. It also has a zoo, a butterfly park, and a safari park.

2. Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park

Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park is a popular attraction that features a musical fountain show with colorful lights and music. It also has a children's play area and a rose garden.

Malls in Bengaluru

1. Orion Mall

Orion Mall is one of the largest and most popular malls in Bengaluru. It has a wide range of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options, including a multiplex, a bowling alley, and a gaming zone.

2. Phoenix Marketcity

Phoenix Marketcity is another huge and modern mall in Bengaluru. It offers a variety of brands, cuisines, and leisure activities, such as a cinema, an amphitheater, and a live music venue.

3. Mantri Square Mall

Mantri Square Mall is one of the oldest and busiest malls in Bengaluru. It has over 250 stores, 39 food outlets, and 6 movie screens. It also has a metro station connected to it.
